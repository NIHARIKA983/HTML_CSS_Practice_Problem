Cardamom

Cardamom, sometimes cardamon or cardamum, is a spice made from the seeds of several plants in the genera Elettaria and Amomum in the family Zingiberaceae.

Black Pepper

Black pepper is a flowering vine in the family Piperaceae, cultivated for its fruit, known as a peppercorn, which is usually dried and used as a spice and seasoning.

Bird chilly

It is high in Capsaicin. It is very effective in controlling cholesterol levels. Traditionally these chilies are also used for reducing blood pressure.
